Why choose Tune'em as your LMS?

  • Modernize traditional learning

    Use a blended approach to learning through Class Room Training (online and offline), Adaptive learning and E-learning microcourses

    Our platform leverages actionable insights from real-time evaluations to drive performance and improve team readiness

  • Role-plays

    Practice makes perfect and with role-plays your teams are ready to go before they get in front of customers

    Give your reps a safe place to practice pitches, demos, and new product messaging with role-plays and empower their managers to give them actionable feedback

  • Establish a community

    With Leadership Speak, enterprises can broadcast Multimedia messages from senior executives to all or defined users

    Users have the option to "like" or "comment" on the posts, giving executives a real-time sense of how the organisation is responding to their messages

    Our analytics Review and aggregate coaching data to track each rep’s scores and identify areas for remediation.
